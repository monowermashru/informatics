A Few Travel Stories

From quiet valleys to busy beaches, and snowy mountains to ancient towns, my journeys across Bangladesh and Japan have shown me amazing places and new experiences. Every trip brought something unique and unforgettable.

A sunrise at Sajek Valley

Sajek Valley is a peaceful hill station in Bangladesh, known as the "Roof of Rangamati." Surrounded by green hills and floating clouds, it feels like walking in the sky.

Niigata's Winter Wonderland

Skiing in Niigata was both breathtaking and intense. The snowy mountain views were stunning, but the steep slopes and cold winds made it a thrilling and risky adventure.
